 split_r2d.hRoutines for splitting convex or non-convex MatPoly's by a cutting plane. Uses R2D. A convex MatPoly will be split into two MatPoly's. A non-convex MatPoly will be decomposed into convex polyhedra using its centroid, several MatPoly's could be returned for each half-plane. All the faces of the input MatPoly are assumed to be planar and no additional facetization is performed, so when sequentially splitting of a faceted MatPoly, no additional facets will be introduced

All the provided material polygons are cut with the same plane. The unique position of the plane is determined based on the aggregate moments of the components in the lower half-space (below the plane)
