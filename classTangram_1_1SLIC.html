inline

Used iterative methods tolerances.

Returns
Tolerances for iterative methods, here ims_tols_[0] correspond to methods for volumes and ims_tols_[1] are NOT used. In particular, ims_tols_[0].arg_eps is a negligible change in cutting distance, ims_tols_[0].fun_eps is a negligible discrepancy in volume.
